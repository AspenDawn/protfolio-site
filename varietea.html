Project Overview

What is VarieTea? VarieTea is a recipe web app centered around tea. This recipe web application contains recipes for tea blends, other tea based drinks and. This web application strives to make tea accessible to a variety of people, from tea enthusiasts to novices and beginners.

The Problem

Tea has been on the rise since 2020 however, the majority of non-alcoholic beverage recipes web apps cater predominately to coffee drinkers. Tea can be just as versatile as coffee in beverage recipes and more!

The Solution

Create a web app that demonstrates and explains how to make tea blends, other tea based beverages, and make tea infused baked goods. The recipes should be presented in a friendly, easy-to-understand manner to appeal to both novice and experienced users.

A/B Test

This test was to see which layout users preferred for organizing their saved recipes and folders. Version A’s layout allows the user to view individual recipes and then toggle to the folder feature. Version B lets the user view both folders and individual recipes in one screen. The results were that users preferred Version B over Version A. They liked that they could view both recipe collections and individual recipes on one screen.

Project Reflection

  • VarieTea was a passion project. There aren’t very many websites or apps that cater specifically to tea and those that do were a bit intimidating to read.
  • Researching recipe web-apps and tea blogs really helped inform what VarieTea should look like and the kind of information to include in it.
  • Periodical reiterations of researching, designing, testing the design and revising based on the results of the test were really important to make VarieTea a usable and enjoyable recipe app.
