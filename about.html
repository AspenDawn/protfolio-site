A bit about me.

My name is Aspen Dawn Concha and I enjoy creating UI designs that are useful and have distinct personalities. I like learning new things and taking inspiration from unexpected and expected places such as video essays, books, movies, random blogs,and just being open to random experiences.

I ultimately became interested in the world of UI when looking for a new challenge and after looking at a few resources, I decided to take a UI course with Careerfoundry to get a better understanding of the overall process and found it to be just the kind of design puzzle I was looking for.
